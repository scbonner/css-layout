Greetings

Come in and dine with us. We grow our own herbs and garden vegetables on three acres right in our backyard.

Also, our meats are purchased from local farmers- can you believe that! Well it is true. We believe in and support our community through networking fostered through food partnerships. Any time we get the chance, we gladly advertise those in the community, notated on the back of our menu. Again, come in and enjoy our delightful fresh food.

Each recipe has you the customer in mind. We only use vegetables that are in season, so our menu is forever changing to please you.. Our choice of meat, also, purchased from local farms of grain-fed animals. We hope that by growing most of our ingredients and purchasing local allows us to keep our prices low and support our local community.
